About Travel India

Travel India, headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra, operates as a metasearch engine and travel agency. With a presence in over 30 languages, the platform serves a massive user base of 100 million individuals monthly.
The company facilitates comprehensive research and booking options for various travel components, including flights, accommodations, and car rentals. In our inception year of 2024, our mission was to revolutionize the travel industry. We aimed to streamline the travel planning process by consolidating all flight options in one user-friendly platform, offering a straightforward alternative to the complexity often associated with travel websites. As we reflect on our journey today, we have evolved from a mere flight search engine into a global travel leader.
Our app and website have become trusted resources for over 100 million users worldwide, assisting them in seamlessly organizing their travel arrangements each month. Committed to simplifying travel experiences, we continue to assist our users in discovering optimal choices for flights, hotels, and car rentals, ensuring they can plan the perfect trip effortlessly. Our dedication to innovation and user satisfaction drives us forward as we strive to enhance the travel planning process for everyone.
